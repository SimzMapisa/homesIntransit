Safe and Secure Storage.

Whether you need to store furniture, fine art, commercial goods or professional equipment, our secure storage facilities are equipped with the best technology to keep your belongings safe.

Designated Storage Warehousing.

With purpose-built storage warehousing we are able to deliver storage with the protection of your belongings at the forefront. With a variety of different storage options available we offer storage solutions for every client, whether you have a single item that requires storage or the content of your property between a national or international home move or a collection of a large oversized items.

For long and short term storage we will carry out a full inventory, making it easy for you to manage what you have in store and should you require all or part of your contents out of storage. You have peace of mind that our storage is temperature controlled and protected by CCTV with motion and sound detection as well as intruder & fire alarms which are monitored remotely 24/7.

Making Our Storage Work For You.

With no minimum contract length, we provide a storage option for all needs. Whether your removal dates have not lined up or you are still awaiting visas for your international relocation these are just a number of reasons that you may require our storage services. We also store plenty of artwork and collections on a long-term basis with our inventory system making it easier to manage what you have in store with us.

Our containerised storage makes it easy to manage your belongings and keep your items together. This modular way of storing also ensures that you only pay for the space that you need, as your volume decreases, the number of containers that you use decreases. Our storage facilities have large indoor loading areas to protect from the elements when loading and unloading.
