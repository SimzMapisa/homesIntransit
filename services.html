Our Services

We will go the extra mile to accommodate special requirements for your relocation. What We Love Doing.

Home Furniture Relocations

Our team of experts will handle every step of the domestic removal process, no matter the volume or the destination of your move. All your household items will be carefully packed with our innovative packing materials and loaded with care into our clean and regularly inspected trucks.

Office Relocations

Corporate removals need meticulous planning, at HomesInTransit our experienced team will be able to offer a bespoke national or international moving schedule for your company or employees.

Vehicle Transfers

HomesInTransit offers door to door vehicle moving services for corporate clients and individuals. We strive to provide a world class vehicle moving service whatever the type: utility vans, sedan, hatchback or SUV, luxury cars, vintage motorbikes, caravans etc.

Specialists Services

From a single painting that needs moving across South Africa, to an entire collection that requires relocating around the world, HomesInTransit has the skill and experience to facilitate this. We also offer packaging, pre and post-cleaning services upon request.

Secure Storage

There are many reasons you may need to use storage, you may be between house moves or need to store your possessions whilst working abroad. Maybe you are renovating a property or undergoing some building work. We offer a convenient and cost effective solution.
