Home Furniture Moves.

Our team of experts will handle every step of the home furniture removal process, no matter the volume or the destination of your move. All your household items will be carefully packed with our innovative packing materials and loaded with care into our clean and regularly inspected trucks.

Hand-crafted Home Moving.

HomesInTransit offers a premium removals experience for residents moving to or from Johannesburg, Capetown, Durban, Port Elizabeth and the surrounding areas. If you are a seasoned mover that relocates on a regular basis or somebody who has lived in the family home for a lifetime, our specialised tailored services are designed to take the weight off your shoulders when moving home.

When carrying out home relocation or renovation projects, we offer short and long term storage solutions in our high-quality storage facilities. Our warehouses are temperature controlled, CCTV and security controlled as well as state of the art management systems. Our oversized storage area enables us to store the largest and most complex pieces.

The Right Team For Your Move.

Each of our moves has its very own moving coordinator. This is your one point of contact who will be making sure your move runs like a Swiss timepiece. Firstly, by arranging an onsite survey with one of our experienced surveyors to assess the details and requirements of your move and the services provided by HomesInTransit. With this information, your surveyor will work with your move coordinator to create a detailed move plan to cover every aspect of your move, including :

  • ✔ Packing and unpacking services.
  • ✔ Arranging parking suspensions.
  • ✔ Determining the correct materials, people and vehicles for your move.
  • ✔ Having specialist crates made for your move for items such as artwork, sculptures and chandeliers.
  • ✔ Planning is the key to any move, but knowledge and experience are what really pays. They know how to overcome difficult access issues and fully expect things to crop up on the day of the move.

Specific Moving Requirements.

You may have specific requirements for your move, this is where we really come into our own. We offer a wide range of specialist services tailor made for you.

Specialist Equipment

Heavier items often require equipment designed to assist with moving them. We have the right equipment to safely move pianos and safes carefully out of a property as well as the right tools to move grandfather clock to their new home. We have a range of vehicles, some with tail lifts to take the weight of those heavier items, as well as the option of chilled vehicles for your wine collection.

Tight Spaces

Getting access to your home is an issue that we come across regularly. We may need to use smaller vehicles to get up close to your property to reduce the loading and unloading time. Specialist lifting equipment and external hoists to remove those tricky, larger items to avoid stairwells or even dismantling and rebuilding items with our handyman service when needed.

Time Sensitive Moves

You may have a specific schedule that you need your move to keep to and this may be for a variety of reasons. Your move coordinator will make this a reality with the assistance of our large source of trained move teams and bespoke removal vehicles.

Confidential Moves

The nature of the move itself may not be known to all employees at the point of quoting, just mention this to your coordinator and we can carry out an out of hours survey or simply be discreet.

The highest level of protection for your belongings.

Your belongings are priceless no matter what their monetary value, so we only use the best materials to safely move your items. Those materials can vary between different items, depending on their surface, humidity requirements or shape. We have many different packing options to suit your requirements.

Packing up your belongings and ensuring their safe movement is an art. We can tailor our packing services to suit your requirements. Our full packing services encompass the packing of the entire content of your property; however, we understand that some people may sometimes wish to partially pack, therefore opting for our fragile packing service just to assist with those trickier and ornate items. Our fully trained packing teams can pack specialist items that require that extra experience and attention to detail from your antique grandfather clock to your collection of fine wine.

Unpacking

As with the packing there are multiple ways in which we can complete your unpacking. Consistency is key with unpacking, ensuring that we have the same team on your unpacking that carried out the packing and move.
